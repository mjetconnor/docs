TimeZone Class

NOTE:  There is no convenient ISO standard for time zones.  Time zone values will be formally defined in this spec, or a related spec, in a way that is as broadly applicable as possible.  At minimum, standard values will be defined that relate to simple constant offsets from UTC ("Coordinated Universal Time", essentially the same as GMT, "Greenwich Mean Time").  Most time zones in practical use also involve a daylight savings time aspect, which is the part that makes standards definitions tricky.
