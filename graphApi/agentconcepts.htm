Agent Concepts

The FOAF (Friend Of A Friend) data model is an RDF vocabulary that describes core concepts related to users, people and system actors in general.  FOAF is a W3C recommended standard.  It is already widely adopted by such social networking players as Facebook and Twitter.  The MindjetGraph set of user and user-related classes conform very closely to FOAF with only minor additions.

The center of this model are the Agents.  Agents are actors in the system; things that do stuff.  A well known sub-class is Person, representing people.  Other kinds of agents are Applications, Organization and even Groups of agents.  Users are a kind of Person, specifically those persons who have UserAccounts.  Note that a given account can be associated with more than one user.  These can be thought of as different profiles for the same person.
