Process Management Concepts

The root element for process management in MindjetGraph is the Assignment.  (These are sometimes called "tasks" in other vocabularies, but that term can be overloaded with many conflicting meanings.  In MindjetGraph, we go with the safer term "assignment".)

All assignments have creator and created properties.  When assignments are entities, these will be those properties that are already defined in that way.  status is also defined to have values that indicate the status of the assignment, such as "pending", "open" or "closed".

Classes in this diagram: Agent, Assignment, DateTime, EntityStatus, Thing

Every assignment also has a dateDue and assignedTo property.  The dateCompleted property is added when the assignment is completed.

If an assignment has an inSupportOf property, this indicates that completion of the assignment somehow furthers the development of that thing.  If that value is an Idea, completion of the assignment relates to that ideaâ€™s evolution, possibly impacting its lifecycle.

assignedTo and inSupportOf both have defined inverse properties, to help track who has assignments.
