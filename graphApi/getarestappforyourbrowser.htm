Get a REST app for your browser

If you don't have one already, you should find a good app or extension for your browser that you can use to manually execute HTTP queries with specific methods and header values.  This is a handy way to try out the queries discussed in this tutorial in a light-weight interactive way.

You will probably find a good app by going to the app store for your browser and searching for "rest client" or "http client".  If you are using Chrome, we have found that the "Postman - REST Client" works well.  But really any will do.  "Your mileage may vary."
