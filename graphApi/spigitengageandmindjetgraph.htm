Focus on SpigitEngage

This help doc, taken as a whole, describes a landscape of data that is not limited to SpigitEngage. Except for parts of the tutorial, the data model concepts and class definitions are not restricted to what SpigitEngage does today, but also apply to future versions of SpigitEngage as well as future enterprise social innovation applications yet to be conceived and developed.  But for now, SpigitEngage is the primary source of Mindjet Graph data.

The section is the only section that discusses and defines the elements of the Mindjet Graph API as they are specifically served by SpigitEngage data sources.

SpigitEngage is an enterprise innovation platform designed to encompass the full breadth and scope of an enterprise scale innovation program.  The application is cloud based, leverages social networking and gaming technologies to engage the crowd in innovation.  SpigitEngage provides a sophisticated model for graduating ideas based on popularity, engagement, expert review, and evaluations.
