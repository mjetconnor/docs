Collaboration Class

Concepts: Collaboration Concepts

Collaboration is a broad concept.  Even so, there is considerable convergence on the essential elements of any collaboration -- a declaration of group of participants, the purpose of the collaboration, and some notion of what success looks like.  The group of participants could be as broad as “absolutely everybody” and as limited as two or three hand-picked friends.  The purpose and success criterion might be inherent in the various subclasses of Collaboration, such as a Question or a Brainstorm.
