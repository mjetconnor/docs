PostalAddress Class

Concepts: Contact Info Concepts

Postal addresses from around the globe can be cleanly represented by this structure.  The expected and legal values for the region and code attributes depend on the country.  For instance, regions in the US are the states, in Ireland are counties.  Country values are ISO standard values, as defined by the Country datatype.
