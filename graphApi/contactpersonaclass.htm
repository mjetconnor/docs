ContactPersona Class

Concepts: Contact Info Concepts

A kind of contact information that is essentially a separate profile.  By virtue of being a subclass of Agent, personas may have distinct names, descriptions, and their own list of contact points.  The different personas are associated with a certain role, such as "personal" or "office.
