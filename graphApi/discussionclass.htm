Discussion Class

Concepts: Comment and Forum Concepts

To keep comment threads in the Mindjet data model as close as possible to the SIOC standard, we organize comments into a kind of sioc:Forum called a “discussion”.  Discussions are simply comment forums that are maintained within the Mindjet data model.

The thing being discussed is the “object” of the discussion.  These are most likely Mindjet entities such as Ideas, Inquiries or Communities.

then this property exists:
