Assessment Class

Concepts: Assessment Concepts

Captures an act of assessment of a thing.  Typical assessments are votes or reviews.

Defines the nature of the assessment, and the type of value that is expected and legal for the assessment’s valuation.  Also provides the context in which to understand the value of the valuation.
