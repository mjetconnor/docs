PairwiseBallot Class

Concepts: Assessment Concepts

A ballot against which pairwise votes are made.

If ideas are specified in the left and right properties, no inherent preference, order or even display location is implied.  The only expectation is that a rendering of a pairwise vote screen is consistent, with "left" ideas being displayed in a predictable location and "right" ideas being displayed in another predictable location.  It is entirely possible that the same two ideas may appear in a later ballot but reversed.  If so, they MUST also be rendered in their correspondingly reversed locations.
