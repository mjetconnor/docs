Comment and Forum Concepts

This concept is patterned heavily after the SIOC vocabulary - Semantically Interlinked Online Communities - in an effort to make discussion threads as broadly shareable as possible.  The core classes in the MindjetGraph data model are Comment and Discussion, which inherit directly from sioc:Comment and sioc:Forum.

A Discussion is a container for comments.  Comments may be made in reply to other comments, as represented by the replyTo property, and its inverse hasReply.

The discussion's topic property indicates the thing being discussed.  A common example are comment threads about ideas, where the topic would be an Idea entity.  Note that it is legal within the data model for a single object to be the topic of multiple independent discussions, although not all implementations will support that.
